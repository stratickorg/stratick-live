Referral Program

At Stratick, we understand the power of networking and partnerships in the business world. That's why we've designed a referral program to reward you for connecting us with other businesses who could benefit from our services. It's a mutually beneficial opportunity to help your network thrive, and for you to earn valuable rewards in return.

How it works

Refer a Business

If you've experienced success with Stratick's solutions, recommend our services to your fellow businesses, partners, or industry peers.

They Partner with Us

When your referred business becomes a Stratick client and starts benefiting from our services, both you and the referred business receive rewards.

Earn Valuable Rewards

You will receive 10% of the first invoice generated to referred business as a token of our appreciation.

Proven Results

Our track record of delivering measurable results distinguishes us from the competition. Your referral can expect to see improvements in their online presence and business growth.
